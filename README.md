# HEAVN
Unity Editor version 6000.4.0f1 required to open project

## How to Play
Run: WinEXE/heavn.exe 

## Controls
Move - A / D 

Jump - W (hold for higher jump) 

Double Jump - W again in air 

Dash - Space 

Grapple - Press J when in range of a Grip Pole 

Wall Slide - hold into wall while airborne 

Wall Jump - W while wall sliding 

## Stars
Three stars can be collected for each level:
1. Complete the level
2. Collect all collectibles
3. Finish under the time limit

Try to collect all 3 stars

## Tips

Level 2: Spam w for wall jumps

Level 3: Use dash when possible

## Player

Loosely based on this tutorial (https://youtu.be/lIZnIFqai2I?si=432Eg1WhLCU-_A8K) with extra hat abilities manually implemented. GenAI was used for bug-fixing and refactoring. Player sprite and animations are hand drawn. Player sfx are sourced from Bfxr.net and logic was modified from learn unity tutorials. Player collisions were adapted from YouTube tutorials and the unity forums. 

## Levels

Levels have been designed by us. Platform manipulation was learnt through youtube tutorials and modified to fit our game. Level music was sourced through youtube. Tilesets were sourced from the unity store and some were modified/created by us. Level ending was modified from unity learn tutorials and the stat saving was implemented by us assisted by GenAI. Level timer was modified from a YouTube tutorial. 

Level 1 is based around the bunny hat's ability to double jump

Level 2 is based around the frog hat's grapple ability

Level 3 is based around the chicken hat's flying abiluty

## Collectibles and Stars

The assets for these are hand drawn. A tutorial (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y2wMGIjBlIw&list=PLXG1jSmcT-NXIHOPL8cZGXvDEGZ3gzurt&index=8) was used and modified by us to display on the canvas and add a threshold to provide a star given the number of collectibles collected. The stars logic was implemented by us and bug-fixed using GenAI
